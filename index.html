About

The word behind the work.

Opit (опит) is a Bulgarian word that translates roughly to experience, attempt, or experiment. It's the kind of word you use when you're learning by doing—trying something, paying attention, and getting a little better.

Opit Works is built around that idea: treat building as practice. Make small, thoughtful things. Keep the loop tight. Let joy and learning reinforce each other.
